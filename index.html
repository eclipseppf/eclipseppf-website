Frequently Asked Questions

We know you might have questions about car paint protection film, and we're here to help. Here, you'll find answers to the most common questions about our car paint protection films. Whether you're curious about installation, maintenance, or the benefits of PPF, we've got you covered. If you need further assistance, feel free to get in touch with our experts.

What is Paint Protection Film (PPF) and what does it do?

PPF is a transparent, durable film applied to your car's exterior surfaces. It protects the paint from scratches, chips, stains, and other minor damage caused by road debris, environmental contaminants, and everyday wear and tear.

How long does PPF last and does it require maintenance?

PPF typically lasts between 5 to 10 years, depending on the quality of the film and how well it is maintained. Regular washing and occasional waxing will help maintain the film's clarity and protective properties. Avoid abrasive cleaners and brushes to prolong its lifespan.

Will PPF change the appearance of my car’s paint?

High-quality PPF is designed to be nearly invisible and should not alter the color or finish of your car’s paint. In fact, it can enhance the gloss and depth of the paint, giving your car a well-maintained look.

Can PPF be removed without damaging the paint?

Yes, PPF can be safely removed by a professional without causing damage to the underlying paint. The adhesive used for PPF is designed to allow for clean removal, preserving your car’s original finish.

Is PPF worth the investment?

If you want to protect your car’s paint from damage and maintain its value, PPF is a worthwhile investment. It can save you money on paint repairs and touch-ups, and helps keep your car looking new for a longer period.
